Heard That is:

Energetic. Ecclectic. Electric. And a tad eccentric.

Heard That is a Detroit based four piece pop rock cover band.

Lead us to the stage, and we'll lead the charge to the dance floor.

Our setlist spans the best music from the last six decades. Songs that make the hips move, the head bang, and the party happen. With the occasional left turn into madness.
